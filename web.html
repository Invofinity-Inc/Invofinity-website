Workflow







Research
Before even starting on designing the UI for the website, we conduct a proper research on questions like, Who will visit your website? Who are your ideal customer personas? What do they expect?





UI/UX
After researching, we then prepare a top-notch UI design for the website based on what kind of audience will visit this website, and what market it's going to enter.We make sure our UI is the best amongst all the competitors, to stand out.





Estimation
Till now we will have a brief idea about your website, then we will have a estimation for the website based on how big is the website, what features are to be build, how many days it'll need to be developed and scalability of the website.





Development
Now we move on to development stage where we make frequent small meets with you, to keep both of us in touch of each other and we neither compromise on quality nor deadlines.





Testing
After the development is complete we first go with running some unit tests for the website, we improve on what are the test results, then we go with aplha and beta tests before creating its final release.
