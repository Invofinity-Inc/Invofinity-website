Tools





Figma

Figma is a web-based graphics editing and user interface design app. You can use it to do all kinds of graphic design work from wireframing websites, designing mobile app interfaces, prototyping designs, crafting social media posts, and everything in between.

Adobe Xd

Adobe XD is the Adobe prototyping tool for user experience and interaction designers. Adobe XD features are used for creating wireframes, prototypes, and screen designs for digital products such as websites and mobile apps.




Adobe Illustrator

Adobe Illustrator is a professional vector-based design and drawing program. Illustrator allows for the creation of everything from single design elements to entire compositions. Adobe Illustrator is used to create a variety of digital and printed images, including cartoons, charts, diagrams, graphs, logos, and illustrations.

Miro

Miro is the online collaborative whiteboarding platform that enables distributed teams to work effectively together, from brainstorming with digital sticky notes to planning and managing agile workflows. We create concepts, map user stories or customer journeys, or conduct roadmap planning easily, enabling us to focus on delivering the right products for our customers.











Workflow







Research
We perfectly balance user and business needs to create delightful products. For this we ask several questions to our clients as per their requirements and desires.





Inspirations & Ideas
We come up with ideas collectively and implement the best of the ideas. We do a good research in the field where we have to design and find top-notch inspirations.





Sketch
Sketching our ideas either physically or digitally, we begin penning down our ideas and iterating over it. Before finalising any ideas, we take client opionions on it.





Design
Simple, minimal and effective. This is our mantra and method. Clean code, fuss-free interfaces and expert execution, design as it should be.





Multiple versions & Final retouch
After designing we create multiple versions of it, may it be with different color schemes or fonts. Final retouching is done once the client agrees to the design.





Final presentation with Mockup
Finally, we deliver our work with mockups and other requirements of the client.
